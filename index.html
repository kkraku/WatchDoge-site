About WatchDoge

The Watchdoge is a revolutionary low cost hardware cloud-based wallet that allows you to watch over your coins such as Bitcoin, Litecoin and Dogecoin, make quick payments, and check who has sent you funds.

It is a community project started on Reddit by Neon aka Karim Kraku and TheRealChexyre.

It is also the first of many projects by Team Xenon lead by Karim.
